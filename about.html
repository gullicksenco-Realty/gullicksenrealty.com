Our Story

Gullicksen & Co Realty was founded with a simple mission: serve our community with the same integrity and dedication we learned in the military. We believe buying or selling a home is one of life's biggest decisions — and it deserves an agent who treats it that way.

As a Marine our roots are in service. "Semper Fi" isn't just a motto — it's how we do business. Always faithful to our clients, always committed to getting the job done right.

We're not a faceless corporation. We're your neighbors, your fellow veterans, your community members. When you work with Gullicksen & Co, you're family.
