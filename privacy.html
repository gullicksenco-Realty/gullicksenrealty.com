Last updated: April 14, 2026

Introduction

Gullicksen Realty ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, mailing address, and property preferences when you submit forms or contact us.
  • Property Information: Details about properties you're interested in selling or buying.
  • Communication Data: Messages, emails, and SMS communications with our team.
  • Website Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and cookies.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide real estate services and respond to your inquiries
  • To send property updates, market reports, and relevant information
  • To send SMS/text messages with your consent (Reply STOP to opt out)
  • To improve our website and services
  • To comply with legal obligations

SMS Communications

By providing your phone number, you consent to receive SMS messages from Gullicksen Realty. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP. Reply HELP for assistance.

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Real estate agents and brokers involved in your transaction
  • Service providers who assist our operations (with confidentiality agreements)
  • Legal authorities when required by law

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.
